What {{ student_name }} Will Experience

The Math Diagnostic is an adaptive diagnostic — similar in format to the digital SAT — that measures four dimensions of mathematical thinking:

F Fluency — Can they execute procedures accurately and efficiently?
U Understanding — Do they grasp the "why" behind the math?
A Application — Can they use math in real-world contexts?
R Reasoning — Can they think through novel problems logically?

The assessment has two modules (about {{ time_minutes }} minutes total). The second module adapts based on how {{ student_name }} does in the first — so every student gets questions matched to their level.
